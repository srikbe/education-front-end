2010 School Improvement Grant Recipients

In 2010, the United States Congress approved nearly $3.5 billion to be disbursed to state governments to improve chronically underperforming schools through the School Improvement Grant (SIG).
Grant funds for each school were contingent on an agreement by the local education agency to implement a designated school "turnaround" model.
The following chart depicts the number of the schools in each state that were awarded SIG funds and organizes this information by geographical region (North, South, Midwest, and West).
